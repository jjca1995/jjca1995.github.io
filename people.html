Gary Yourofsky is a vegan activist that travels the world giving speeches about the importance of veganism. Click here to visit his website to hear more about his activism.

Dr. Colin T. Campbell is the author of "The China Study", the comprehensive 20 year study on diet and nutrition. Click here to learn more about his life's work

Dr. McDougall is the author of "The Starch Solution", and he is one of the leading doctors in plant based nutrition. His research has helped people lose weight, and helped diabetics reduce, or stop needing their medication. Click here to learn more about his research.
