The Dairy Industry

The video above shows the true cost of milk. Our need for milk, cheese, ice cream, etc. costs its mother the loss of her baby just hours after birth.

Everyone has probably heard that we must drink milk to get strong bones, but does dairy actually help build stronger bones? The US has the highest consumption of dairy in the world, and we also have the highest rate of Osteoporosis.

Studies have shown that milk acutally increases calcium loss in the bones. The animal protein found in milk increases the body pH. Calcium is an acid reducer, and the biggest storage of calcium is found in the bones, which causes a net calcium loss.

Drinking plant based milks provide you with the same aount of calcium as regular milk, but since it does not contain animal proteins, the body does not lose calcium
